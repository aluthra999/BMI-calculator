What is BMI?

BMI stands for Body Mass Index, a numerical value calculated from your height and weight. It's a widely used tool to assess if your body weight falls within a healthy range. Your BMI can help you determine whether you are underweight, at a healthy weight, overweight, or obese.

How to Use Our BMI Calculator

Using our BMI calculator is quick and easy. All you need to do is:
1. Enter your weight in kilograms.
2. Enter your height in centimeters.
3. Click the "Calculate" button.

The calculator will then provide you with your BMI value and a corresponding classification to help you understand where you stand in terms of your weight.

FAQs

Your BMI falls into one of the following categories:
  • Underweight: A BMI below 18.5
  • Healthy Weight: A BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: A BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: A BMI of 30 or above

Remember, while BMI is a useful screening tool, it has some limitations. It doesn't take into account factors like muscle mass, bone density, and overall body composition. Therefore, it's essential to consider other factors, such as your lifestyle, activity level, and health history.

Your BMI can provide valuable information about your health, but it should be just one piece of the puzzle. Consult with a healthcare professional to discuss your BMI results and get personalized advice on maintaining a healthy weight and lifestyle.
